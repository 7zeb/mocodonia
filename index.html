Welcome to Project Mocodonia

Mocodonia is a colonization server made by 7Zeb, started in November 2022. It has had cities and more built.

Discover the creativity and collaboration of players as they build cities like Toniron, the capital of Zuik.

Explore, join, and shape the history of Mocodonia yourself!

Featured City: Toniron, Zuik

Toniron is the capital of Zuik, and the oldest city in Mocodonia. A historic and bustling hub of activity.

  • Founded: November 2022
  • Region: Zuik
  • Status: Capital City
